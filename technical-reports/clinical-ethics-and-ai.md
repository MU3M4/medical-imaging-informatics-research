# Executive Summary: Ethics in Medical AI Implementation

**Overview:** A research-based analysis of the regulatory hurdles facing AI-driven diagnostics in modern healthcare.

**Key Findings:**
* **Data Privacy:** Navigating the tension between large-scale data harvesting and individual patient rights.
* **Algorithmic Bias:** The necessity of diverse "Ground Truth" datasets to prevent diagnostic errors across different demographics.
* **Accuracy Standards:** Why human-in-the-loop (HITL) verification is the only way to reach the 99.9% accuracy required for clinical deployment.
